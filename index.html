About

My name is Negar (Niki) Sadeghi. I am a PhD student in Industrial Engineering at Northeastern University, where I work on simulation based optimization and AI driven decision support for healthcare and pharmaceutical supply chains.

My research lies at the intersection of optimization, simulation, and machine learning, with a focus on time sensitive and patient specific systems such as cell and gene therapy supply chains. I develop data driven models to analyze uncertainty, operational tradeoffs, and system level performance in complex healthcare logistics problems.

I am happy to connect and discuss research ideas or potential collaborations.
